## Brief
Premium dark-theme website for an AI automation agency targeting hospitality businesses in Melbourne and Adelaide. Navy (#0a0f1e) and gold (#c9a84c) palette. Tagline: "Keeping you ahead of the curve."

## Idea 1 — Precision Noir

**Design Movement:** Brutalist Luxury / High-End Fintech Dark

**Core Principles:**
1. Stark contrast — near-black navy backgrounds with razor-sharp gold type
2. Structural asymmetry — offset grid columns, left-anchored headings
3. Data-driven elegance — thin rule lines, monospaced accents, measured spacing
4. Restrained animation — content slides in from the left, gold underlines draw on scroll

**Color Philosophy:** The navy is a void — a space of infinite possibility. Gold is precision — the exact moment where human effort becomes automated output. White is clarity. No mid-tones.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column grid where headings anchor left and body text floats right. Section dividers use thin gold horizontal rules. Full-bleed canvas hero.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold rule lines that animate in on scroll
- Monospaced eyebrow labels (uppercase, tracked)
- Offset card grid with gold left-border accent

**Interaction Philosophy:** Purposeful. Hover states reveal gold underlines. Buttons have a subtle fill-sweep animation from left to right.

**Animation:** Framer Motion staggered fade-up on section entry. Canvas particle system in hero. Gold rule lines draw in from left.

**Typography System:** Headings — Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis). Body — DM Sans (400/500). Eyebrows — Space Mono (uppercase, tracked).

## Idea 2 — Kinetic Gold (SELECTED)

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Dark Luxury / Motion-First Brand Identity

**Core Principles:**
1. Motion as meaning — every animation tells the story of transformation (chaos → precision)
2. Gold as hierarchy — only the most important elements receive gold treatment
3. Depth through layering — cards float above the navy void with subtle glow halos
4. Typographic contrast — ultra-bold display headings against light body text

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0a0f1e) is the canvas — a premium, almost-black space that feels like a high-end product box. Gold (#c9a84c) is the reward — used sparingly so it always commands attention. White text is clean and authoritative.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with canvas animation. Below: alternating asymmetric sections with wide left margins. Cards use a subtle glass-morphism treatment (dark translucent with gold border glow). Stats bar uses a horizontal marquee feel.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold gradient glow behind key headings
- Cards with `border-gold/20` and `shadow-gold/10` hover glow
- Section entry animations with staggered children

**Interaction Philosophy:** Responsive and rewarding. Gold CTA button has a shimmer sweep on hover. Section cards lift slightly on hover. Smooth scroll with intersection observer triggers.

**Animation:** Canvas water-to-particle hero. Framer Motion fade-up + slide-up stagger on all sections. Gold shimmer on CTA button. Pendulum logo swing.

**Typography System:** Headings — Cormorant Garamond (bold, display). Sub-headings — Outfit (600). Body — Outfit (400). Eyebrows — Outfit (500, uppercase, tracked 0.15em).

## Idea 3 — Architectural Minimal

**Design Movement:** Swiss Grid Meets Dark Mode / Corporate Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. Grid discipline — 12-column strict alignment, no decorative overflow
2. Negative space as luxury — generous padding signals premium positioning
3. Typographic hierarchy — size and weight do all the work
4. Micro-detail obsession — pixel-perfect spacing, consistent 8px grid

**Color Philosophy:** Navy is the foundation. Gold is used only for interactive states and key data points. Everything else is white or white/50 on dark.

**Layout Paradigm:** Centered max-width container with strict column alignment. All sections follow the same structural template. Clean, predictable, trustworthy.

**Signature Elements:**
- Numbered section labels (01, 02, 03)
- Gold dot indicators in lists
- Hairline borders between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:** Invisible until needed. Hover states are subtle color shifts. Focus states use gold ring.

**Animation:** Minimal — fade-in only. No distracting motion.

**Typography System:** Headings — Inter Display (800). Body — Inter (400). All one font family, varied weights.
